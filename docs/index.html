Do participants need any creative experience?+
Absolutely not! Our workshops are designed for everyone ‚Äî from people who "can't even draw a stick figure" to seasoned artists. We provide all the guidance, materials, and creative prompts. Everyone leaves with something they're proud of.

We bring everything ‚Äî vintage magazines, art supplies, scissors, glue, specialty papers, recipe cards, craft tools, and all facilitator materials. All you need is a space with tables and chairs. We handle setup and cleanup too.

Standard workshops are 2-2.5 hours, which gives everyone time to settle in, create, and share. We can adjust timing for your schedule ‚Äî from 90-minute sessions to half-day creative retreats.
Can you come to our office / venue?+
Yes! We come to you. Office, restaurant, retreat venue, rooftop ‚Äî wherever your team gathers. We've set up in conference rooms, co-working spaces, backyards, and even parks. Currently serving the Los Angeles area with travel available for larger events.
Can we customize the theme?+
Absolutely. We've done holiday parties, product launch celebrations, new hire onboarding experiences, and quarterly offsites with custom themes. Tell us your vision and we'll design around it.
What's the Effemera zine?+
Effemera started as a physical zine ‚Äî a beautiful printed publication about slowing down, cooking, crafting, and preserving moments. Each issue includes recipe cards, bingo cards, conversation starters, and collage pages. The workshops grew from the same philosophy: create more than you consume.
