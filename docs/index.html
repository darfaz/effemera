FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do participants need creative experience?+
Not at all! Our workshops are designed for everyone ‚Äî from people who "can't even draw a stick figure" to seasoned artists. We provide all the guidance, materials, and prompts. Everyone leaves with something they're proud of.
What do you bring vs. what do we provide?+
We bring everything ‚Äî vintage magazines, art supplies, scissors, glue, specialty papers, recipe cards, craft tools, and all facilitator materials. All you need is a space with tables. We handle setup and cleanup too.
How long are the workshops?+
Standard workshops are 2-2.5 hours. We can adjust timing ‚Äî from 90-minute sessions to half-day creative retreats.
Can you come to our location?+
Yes! We come to you ‚Äî offices, restaurants, retreat venues, community centers, even parks. Currently based in NYC with travel available for larger events.
How is this different from art therapy?+
While art therapy involves a licensed therapist, Effemera focuses on active, guided participation in a welcoming group setting. We create an environment where the act of making things together ‚Äî the cutting, gluing, arranging, sharing ‚Äî naturally creates connection and calm. No clinical framework needed.
Do you work with recovery programs?+
Absolutely. We're bringing our creative workshops to recovery facilities, halfway houses, and women's wellness programs. Crafting provides a safe, grounding activity that supports the healing journey. We offer flexible pricing for nonprofits and community organizations.
Can we customize the theme?+
Yes! We've designed experiences for holiday parties, product launches, onboarding days, and quarterly offsites with custom themes. Tell us your vision and we'll build around it.
